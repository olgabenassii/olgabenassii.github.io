About us

Good Morning!
We are Benassi Olga, Bruno Federica, Carretti Elisa, Curcio Samuele and Tsoi Ying Lam Sandy; we are a group of Language, Communication and Society students in the University of Bologna.

We would like to explain to you a bit about our project.
Our aim was to explore and enrich the ArCo ontology of Italian culture through SPARQL queries, and to assess whether we could find new elements to add to the knowledge already present.
In order to do that, We used a set of tools, such as:

    ChatGPT
    Gemini
    Llama
    Mixtral


These LLMs (Large Language Models) were used as a support in our work of building SPARQL queries to run in the ArCo website (See more here).
